MIMHO Airdrop

The Airdrop module distributes MIMHO tokens to the community based on predefined rules, eligibility and on-chain verification.

Purpose

Reward early users, contributors and strategic participants in a fair and transparent way.

Security

Eligibility rules and claims are enforced entirely on-chain, preventing abuse or double claims.

Integration

Integrated with staking, score, DAO decisions, vesting logic and ecosystem events.
