MIMHO Locker

Purpose

Provide a public, neutral, and trust-minimized way to lock tokens for teams, communities, and individual users‚Äîwithout depending on third parties.

Security

Locks are enforced on-chain: no early unlocks, no manual withdrawals, no hidden backdoors. The release is automatic after the programmed time.

Fees in MIMHO

Users pay a percentage fee in MIMHO to create a lock. This makes the Locker sustainable as a public utility and aligns usage with the ecosystem token.
