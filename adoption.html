Adopt a Martian

A global adoption page where verified animal NGOs can list pets available for adoption — with breed, age, city, photos, and official contact links. Built to be transparent, safe, and easy to use.

ADOPT MODULE IMAGE

What you will find here

Verified adoption listings: breed, age, city, photos, and direct contact links to the NGO — so you can adopt with confidence.

How it will work

Pages will load up to 20 pets per page, with next/previous arrows for browsing. The goal is speed, clarity, and low friction.

Safety and verification

Only verified NGOs will be able to publish listings. This reduces spam and protects adopters from fake listings.

Status

This page is live as a preview. Listings will appear as partnerships go live and verification is enabled.
