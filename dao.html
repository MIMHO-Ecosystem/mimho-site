Purpose

Turn community decisions into on-chain actions: proposals, votes, parameter changes, and secure control transitions across the ecosystem.

Security

Governance is enforced by smart contracts with strict rules, public events, and non-custodial design. No hidden admin actions.

Every proposal, vote, and result is publicly auditable. The ecosystem broadcasts critical actions through public on-chain events.
